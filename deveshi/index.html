Our Products

We deal with all kinds of Electronic Weighing Scale, Standard Weights, Weighbridge, Checkweighers, Field Instruments (Sensors, Switches, Micro Switches, VFD, etc.)

Floor Scale

Four load cell based heavy duty floor scale designed for different industrial weighing application in food,pharma and other segments.The capacity ranges from 300kg to 5000kg.

Weighbridge

The Weighbridge Truck Scale has been manufactured to the highest standards and will provide you with a long term solution. The truck scale has been designed and verified by suitably qualified weighing and structural engineers.

Batching System

The batch controller combines advanced batching control technology with the powerful features of the IND780 terminal.It works as a stand-alone terminal for single scale, multi scale, or flow meter applications.

Tank and Vessel Weighing System

Tank weighing system normally consists of load cells, junction box and weighing controller. It is widely used in production management and process control areas where weight measurement & control is involved.

ICS Compact Scale

An optimal combination between terminal and weighing platform leads to less production failure and speeds up your productivity.Cover all requirements of your customer/regulatory body - possible with our user management, alibi memory, routine test and calibration log feature.

Low Profile Scale

The low overall height of 35mm- 45mm makes the low-profile scales ideal for floor installations with approach ramps - no costly pit installation!

IND 235

IND 235 terminals provide a compact yet flexible solution for a variety of industrial weighing requirements.The stainless scale offers IP67 protection for wet and harsh environments, while a large 40mm backlit display offers excellent readability.

IND 215

The IND 215 is a fully stainless steel terminal with IP67 protection. Four front panel key navigation makes it easy to operate. It also offers an inbuilt RS232 port for easy PC/Printer connectivity.

BPA 121 Compact Scale

Ensures longevity in wet environments, reduces maintenance work and downtime, supports fast weighing, and improves efficiency.Long battery operating time and intuitive three-color indicators for battery volume are helpful in reducing downtime
